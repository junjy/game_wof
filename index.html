HOW TO PLAY

This basic 'Hangman' word puzzle game is based on the popular Wheel of Fortune gameshow with some minor variations.

1. This is a single-player game.

2. You can play up to 3 rounds per game.
- Your goal is to guess all 3 word puzzles without spinning 'BANKRUPT' or making a wrong guess.

3. At the start of each round, you can only Spin the Wheel.
- Thereafter, you can choose any of the following 3 options:-

1. Spin the Wheel
- Spin the wheel and guess a consonant by typing the letter in the input field.

2. Buy a Vowel
- Each vowel costs $250 (flat rate).
- To guess the vowel, type the letter in the input field.

3. Solve the Puzzle
- You may choose to solve the puzzle at any time.
- Type your answer in full in the input field, including spaces and special characters as shown in the puzzle.

4. You will lose the round/game if any of the following occur:-
- If you spin 'BANKRUPT' or guess an incorrect consonant.
- If you have insufficient money to buy a vowel or guess an incorrect vowel.
- If your guess answer is incorrect.

5. UP THE STAKES!
Challenge yourself by turning on the timed puzzle feature:-
- Guess a Consonant: 5 sec
- Buy a Vowel: 5 sec
- Solve the Puzzle: 10 sec

Have fun!
