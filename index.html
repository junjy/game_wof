HOW TO PLAY

This basic word puzzle game is modelled on the popular Wheel of Fortune gameshow with some minor variations.

1. This is a single-player game.
- There is still a total of 24 values on the wheel (between $300 to $3000 or 'BANKRUPT') but no 'LOSE A TURN' option.

2. You can play up to 3 rounds per game. Each round features 1 word puzzle (phrase or sentence).
- Your goal is to solve all puzzles at the end of 3 rounds without spinning 'BANKRUPT' or making a wrong guess.
- You may exit the game at any time.

3. At the start of each round, you can only Spin the Wheel.
- Thereafter, you can choose any of the following 3 options:-
(1) Spin the Wheel
(2) Buy a Vowel
(3) Solve the Puzzle

Spin the Wheel
- First, spin the wheel to get a dollar value and guess a consonant by typing the letter in the input field.
- Calling a correct letter earns the value multiplied by the number of times the letter appears in the puzzle.

Buy a Vowel
- Before buying a vowel, you must ensure a minimum of $250 in earnings.
- Each vowel costs $250, regardless of the number of times it appears in the puzzle.
- To guess the vowel, type the letter in the input field.

Solve the Puzzle
- You may choose to solve the puzzle at any time without waiting to guess all the letters.
- Type your answer in full in the input field, including any spaces, dashes (-), dots (.), apostrophes (') and other special characters as displayed in the puzzle.

4. Your earnings in that round will be reset to $0 and you will exit the game if any of the following scenarios occur:-
- Spin the Wheel: If flipper falls on 'BANKRUPT' or guessed consonant is incorrect.
- Buy a Vowel: If you have insufficient money to buy a vowel in that round or guessed vowel is incorrect.
- Solve the Puzzle: If or your answer is incorrect or you did not type the answer in full including any special characters (see above).

Your earnings for the previous successful rounds will not be affected.

5. UP THE STAKES!
Challenge yourself by turning on the timed puzzle feature:-
- Guess a Consonant: 5 sec
- Buy a Vowel: 5 sec
- Solve the Puzzle: 10 sec
- Choose each of your next steps (Spin Wheel/Buy a Vowel/Solve the Puzzle): 10 sec.

Have fun!
